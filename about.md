## Artist Bio

Nina McNinch is a printmaker, papermaker, and bookartist that is continuously entranced by the natural world. She uses close-looking of her environment as inspiration for creating detailed etching and mezzotint prints. She has a strong love for tedious processes. Her papermaking practice is very focused on thin and delicate sheets of paper, and deeper interaction with the natural environment.
